Rodney Everhart

In the photograph on the right, Rodney Everhart at his home on Hutchinson Island, FL with an 11'0" East Coast Surfboard. Everhart did grunt work for East Coast Surfboard but later moved on to help Sonny Danner and Herman Pritchard at Dan-Pri Surfboards.

The board pictured on the right is likely only one of two boards of that size that Rodney remembers being made by East Coast and may have originally belonged to Bill Reid. According to Everhart, he refers to it as a "Lank's plank Bill Reid nose-rider model with a Joe Marley speed skeg."

Photos courtesy: Steve Everhart.
